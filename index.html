Be The Change

Mission

Be The Change, a 501(c)(3), conducts activities that better the lives of all. We wish to contribute to a better world by promoting meaningful change. Be The Change is dedicated to fostering environmental stewardship, mentorship, and equitable access to nature. We empower individuals and communities in New England and beyond to connect with the outdoors, preserve natural spaces, and engage in meaningful mentorship that inspires sustainable change for a brighter, more inclusive future.

We have been working with various nonprofits to support their mission including volunteering to support equitable access to environmental educational programs.
